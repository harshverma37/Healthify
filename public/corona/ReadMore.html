Join Us In This Fight Against Covid-19

The COVID 19 pandemic has presented a gigantic challenge to the health system in our country. CMAI as part of the Christian Coalition for Health has offered a letter of support to the Prime Minister to complement the efforts of the Government in stepping up to this challenge.

We are presenting a proposal to the Ministry indicating our level of support for the COVID-19 crisis. We are also exploring possibilities of CSR Funds to support our efforts.

The CMAI Network Hospitals have varied ability to handle the possible surge of COVID-19 cases. But all our healthcare workers will require the basic personal protection in order to attend to the patients.

The challenge that we face are

  1. Lack of supplies of PPE and sanitizers
  2. Lack of disinfectants for environmental sanitation
  3. Shortage of human resource
  4. Lack of adequate equipmemts for oxygen supply
  5. Drastic reduction of revenue in each hospital due to lockdown

Your willingness to help and contribute the key requirements for this fight to our network hospitals is very much appreciated.

We need you to join us in this fight against COVID-19
