Institutional Membership

All our Health Institutions/Programmes which are non-profit, voluntary organizations in nature by virtue of their being registered, either directly or through their parent body und the Societies or Companies or Trust Act provided these Institutions.

Payment of Annual Membership Fees as prescribed by the Association from time to time.

Our member institutions include Christian hospitals, health centres and community programmes. Of these, a substantial number are located in remote and backward areas, taking care of some of the most impoverished and marginalized groups. Some of these institutions have been active for over a century now.

Our membership institutions also include premier teaching institutions like the Christian Medical Colleges at Vellore and Ludhiana.

If you fulfil the criteria and want to apply for Institutional Membership, kindly write to us at cmai@cmai.org.
